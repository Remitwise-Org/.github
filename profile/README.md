RemitWise is a Stellar-based cross-border remittance and financial planning platform designed for migrant workers and their families. Instead of only transferring money, RemitWise enables **smart allocation of remittances into spending, savings, bills, and protection**, with full transparency and automation.

Built on the Stellar ecosystem, RemitWise leverages fast, low-cost payments and fiat on/off-ramps (anchors) to deliver a seamless experience for both senders and recipients.
